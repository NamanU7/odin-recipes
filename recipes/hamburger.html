Hamburger

Description

This is a simple to execute recipe for a nice juicy burger.

It is the perfect recipes for a saturday afternoon lunch with some friends, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 buns

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill
  2. Mix together salt, pepper, and beef and sculpt 1 inch patties
  3. Place patties on the grill, cook 7 minutes each side.
  4. Remove patties and place in between 2 buns, serve while hot.
