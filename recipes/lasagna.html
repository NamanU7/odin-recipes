Lasagna

Description

This recipe is for the lasagna dish. Lasagna sounds fairly hard initially, but this site simplifies the recipe so that anyone can make it.

It takes some work but the result is surely worth the little bit of work. This recipe is perfect for a sunday lunch with the family. It is an authentic Italian recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomato
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. In an oven cook the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over mdeium heat until brown.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook lasagna noodles in water.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  4. Assemble meat and noodles
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
