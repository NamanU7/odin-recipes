Pasta Alfredo

Description

This is a simple to make recipe that yields a staple dish for lucnh or dinner any day of the week!

This dish is perfect for a delicious substitution to our daily lunch. Prepare fast and enjoy fast!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounce pasta
  • 4 tablespoon butter
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmasean cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in boiling water
  2. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium heat and add the garlic
  3. Pour in the milk and cream and stir
  4. Mix in the salt and pepper when the sauce comes to a boil and turn off the heat
  5. Mix in the pasta and let cool for 10 minutes before serving
